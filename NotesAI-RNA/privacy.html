Privacy Policy - NotesAI-RNA

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

NotesAI-RNA respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, stored, and protected when people use the application and related services.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how the product is used, we may collect:

  • Account details such as username and email address
  • Notes, tasks, and other content created inside the product
  • AI prompts, conversations, and related usage history
  • Device, browser, and technical usage information
  • Authentication and account management information

3. How information is used

Collected information may be used to provide the product, improve performance, support syncing, enable collaboration, deliver AI-backed features, and help maintain security.

4. Cloud-backed services

NotesAI-RNA uses cloud-backed services for parts of the user experience. This helps keep the Android package lightweight while still supporting AI and cross-device access.

5. Data storage and security

Reasonable security measures are used to help protect stored information. While no system can promise absolute security, the product is designed to protect user information and reduce unnecessary exposure.

6. Sharing of information

Personal information is not sold. Information may be shared only when required to provide the service, when collaboration features are used by the user, or when required by law.

7. Offline and local data

Some product information may be stored locally on a device to improve continuity and usability. This can help users continue working while reconnecting to online services.

8. User choices

Users may be able to update account details, request support, manage shared access, and contact the team with questions about privacy or account handling.

9. AI disclaimer

AI-generated output may be incomplete or incorrect. Users should review important information before relying on it for study, work, or decision-making.

10. Policy updates

This policy may change over time. Updated versions may be published through the website, application, or other official communication channels.
